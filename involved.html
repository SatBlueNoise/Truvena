Partner With Us

We're seeking partners who believe brilliant minds deserve opportunity regardless of geography.

Join This Work

The AI education gap is widening right now. Every day that passes, more brilliant minds are wasted. We need partners who are willing to do the hard work of reaching the children others ignore.

Foundations & Institutional Funders

We're seeking grant funding from foundations committed to educational equity, AI for social good, and international development. Our focus on "hard places" complements—not competes with—broader AI education initiatives.

What we offer: Rigorous measurement, transparent reporting, focus on underserved communities, sustainable local ownership, and willingness to tackle the hardest implementation challenges.

Current needs: Pilot funding ($75-150K for 2026-2027), expansion funding ($250-500K for 2027-2028), long-term partnership for regional scale.

Explore Partnership

Corporate Partners

If your organization is developing AI education tools, we can be your implementation partner for hard-to-reach markets. You build the technology; we ensure it reaches children in developing countries.

What we offer: On-ground expertise in Central America, relationships with communities and education leaders, proven implementation capacity in resource-constrained environments.

Partnership models: Implementation contracts, joint pilot programs, CSR initiatives, technology adaptation projects.

Discuss Partnership

Individual Donors

Every contribution helps unlock potential. Whether you can fund one student's access to AI tutoring or an entire classroom, your support directly changes lives.

Impact of your donation: $100 provides one student with AI learning tools for a year. $5,000 equips a classroom. $25,000 supports a full school implementation.

Transparency: We'll show you exactly where your dollars go and what outcomes they produce. Real accountability to you and the children we serve.

In-Kind Support

We need more than funding. Technology donations, pro-bono professional services, and strategic partnerships all accelerate our work.

Current needs: Devices (tablets, laptops), internet connectivity solutions, cloud computing credits, translation services, monitoring & evaluation expertise, legal/accounting support.

On-Ground Partners

If you're an NGO, school, or community organization in Central America interested in bringing AI education to your students, we want to hear from you.

What we're looking for: Commitment to educational equity, willingness to pilot new approaches, capacity to support implementation, openness to measurement and evaluation.

What we provide: AI education tools, teacher training, implementation support, outcome measurement, long-term partnership.

Why Partner With Truvena?
