The Truvena AI Governance System™

A comprehensive framework that keeps AI aligned, controlled, and continuously safe.

Why AI Governance Matters

AI goes “rogue” in subtle, real‑world ways: contradicting values, exceeding authority, drifting over time, or producing outputs that erode trust. These are governance failures — and they are preventable.

Pillar 1 — Alignment

Ensure AI starts aligned with your mission, values, and boundaries.

  • AI Values Interview System™
  • AI Constitution
  • Scope & Authority Map
  • Tone & Communication Guide
  • Boundary & Violation Rules
  • Value Robustness Testing

Pillar 2 — Control

Ensure humans stay in charge and AI cannot act beyond its authority.

  • Human‑in‑the‑Loop (HITL) Framework
  • Red Teaming / Adversarial Testing
  • Safety Gate Rollout Plan
  • Kill Switch Protocols
  • Explainability Requirements
  • Privacy & Security Controls

Pillar 3 — Assurance

Ensure AI stays aligned and safe over time.

  • Continuous Monitoring Dashboard
  • Quarterly Alignment Audits
  • Model Update Testing
  • Governance Committee Support
  • Incident Response Protocol
  • Annual Governance Review
