The Truvena AI Governance System™

AI that stays aligned, controlled, and continuously safe.

Pillar 1 — Alignment

Ensure AI starts aligned with your mission, values, and boundaries.

  • AI Values Interview System™
  • AI Constitution
  • Scope & Authority Map

  • Boundary & Violation Rules

Pillar 2 — Control

Ensure humans stay in charge and AI cannot act beyond its authority.

Ensure AI stays aligned and safe over time.

  • Continuous Monitoring Dashboard
  • Quarterly Alignment Audits
  • Model Update Testing
  • Governance Committee Support
  • Incident Response Protocol
  • Annual Governance Review

  • Tier 3 — Enterprise AI Governance Program: All pillars, scaled
